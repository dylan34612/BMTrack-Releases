Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

BristolLog ("the app", "we") is a bowel movement tracking app developed and published by KailzTech. This policy explains what information the app accesses, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Data stored on your device

All bowel movement logs, notes, and analysis outputs are stored only on your device. We do not upload this data to our own servers.

2. Permissions the app requests

  • Camera - used to capture photos for AI-assisted analysis when you choose to use that feature.
  • Internet - required for cloud AI analysis (Firebase AI / Gemini), ads in the free tier (Google AdMob), and optional billing-related checks.

3. AI-assisted analysis (Firebase AI / Gemini)

When you use cloud AI analysis, text and images you provide are sent to Google's Gemini API via Firebase AI Logic to generate results. This processing is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not store the raw request contents on our own servers.

BristolLog can also use an on-device model when available. On-device inference runs locally on your device.

4. User-supplied API key (optional)

If you enable "Use your own Gemini API key," your key is stored locally on your device using Android encrypted storage. Requests are sent directly from your device to Google Gemini and are governed by Google's policies.

5. Firebase Analytics

The app may collect aggregated analytics events to improve product quality and reliability. This data is processed under Google's privacy terms.

6. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free tier can display ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers and usage signals according to Google's advertising policy.

7. KCalTrack integration (optional)

If enabled, BristolLog can read meal context and shared local model metadata from KCalTrack installed on the same device. This integration happens on-device through Android content providers and permissions; BristolLog does not upload this synced data to our own servers.

8. No account required

The app does not require account sign-in. We do not directly collect your identity.

9. Children's privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy over time. The "last updated" date reflects the current version.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact KailzTech through the Play Store listing.

BristolLog is an independent app and is not affiliated with Google.
